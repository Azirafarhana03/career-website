Steps to Becoming a Successful Digital Marketer

  1. Complete SPM with a good result
  2. Enroll in a pre-university or foundation program
  3. Pursue a Bachelor's Degree in New Media Communication or Marketing
  4. Attend digital marketing workshops or certifications (e.g., Google Ads, SEO, Meta Blueprint)
  5. Join internships or part-time jobs in marketing or media fields
  6. Build a portfolio with personal or freelance marketing projects
  7. Stay updated with digital trends, platforms, and tools
  8. Apply for digital marketing jobs or become a freelancer
